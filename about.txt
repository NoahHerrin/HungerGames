This Program is a companion app for hunger games/pvp real life game like nerf wars.

This program aims to:
  -reduce confusion of who is alive,
  -reduce frustration when searching for opponents,
  -added complexity with revive system
    +when somebody dies a timer starts, and depending on how long the game has lasted, their teamates have a
    a specific amount of time to find their teammate and revive them. teamates will recieve a notification with
    a general direction towards their teammates, and a time of how long the teammate has.
  -support for teams
